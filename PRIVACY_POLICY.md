## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Email address** - Used for account creation and authentication
- **Full name** - Used to personalize your experience
- **Phone number** (optional) - Used for account security and notifications
- **Password** - Encrypted and stored securely for account access
- **User role** - Determines your access permissions within the app

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use the app, we automatically collect:
- **Device information** - Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage data** - Features accessed, time spent in app, interaction patterns
- **Location data** (with permission) - Used for field reports and location-based features
- **Log data** - Error logs, performance metrics, crash reports

### Data You Create

- **Field reports** - Reports submitted by field workers
- **Survey responses** - Answers to surveys and questionnaires
- **Analytics interactions** - Data you view and interact with
- **Social media monitoring data** - Public social media content you track

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide services** - Enable core app functionality and features
- **Authenticate users** - Verify your identity and secure your account
- **Personalize experience** - Customize content based on your role and preferences
- **Analyze usage** - Improve app performance and user experience
- **Send notifications** - Alert you to important events and updates
- **Generate insights** - Provide AI-powered analytics and recommendations
- **Ensure security** - Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- **Comply with laws** - Meet legal and regulatory requirements

- **Correction** - Update or correct inaccurate information

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- **Encryption** - Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Access controls** - Role-based permissions and authentication
- **Secure storage** - Data stored in secure, compliant cloud infrastructure
- **Regular audits** - Ongoing security assessments and updates
